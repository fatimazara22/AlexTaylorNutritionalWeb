Eating Habits

Healthy eating includes a balance of vegetables, fruits, lean proteins, whole grains and healthy fats, while limiting the amount of sugar, salt, and saturated/trans fats, eating more whole foods, keeping hydrated with fluids, and staying active to promote physical health, with guidelines encouraging variety and portion control. The main habits include aiming for 5 or more portions of veg/fruit a day, choosing lean proteins, unsaturated oils and reducing processed items high in fat, sugar, and salt.
