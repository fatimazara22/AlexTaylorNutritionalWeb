Exercise

Frequent exercise is crucial for both mental and physical health. It can help to improve heart function, weight, sleep, mood and lowering risks of chronic diseases such as cancer, diabetes and dementia, while suggested routines mix aerobic activity (such as a brisk walk or a cycling session) with additional strength training ( for example resistance bands, weights) and flexibility/balance work (Pilates, yoga) for overall well-being and aiming it for around 150 mins of gentle activity weekly.
